Lionel Messi "The GOAT"

Lionel Messi joined Inter Miami in 2023, changing the face of soccer in the United States.

Messi has won multiple Ballon d'Or awards and is regarded as one of the greatest players of all time.

Messi is known as "La Pulga" (The Flea) and is beloved by fans worldwide including the Brays.
